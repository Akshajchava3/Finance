READ ME
This is my ongoing project making sentiment analysis mainstream!
BeanStock uses Reddit, Yahoo Finance, and other sources to determine a "Sentiment Score" for each ticker on the market, 
denoting them with a "Buy", "Sell", or "Hold" tag.
The point of sentiment analysis is to understand what other people think of a stock on a certain day.
Combining sentiment data with traditional stock analysis tools creates a unique method of discerning popular v. underdog stocks
to influence your trading habits and optimize your investments.
Analyze away!
